What is Physical Health?


Physical health can refers to the absence of illness and sickness. It can include a variety of terms such as balanced diet, weight management, personal hygiene and sleep.

Physical Activities for Different Age

Children (3-5 years)

It is recommended that their do play or do some activities daily and regularly


Examples:-

  • Aerobic Activities

    Running, Walking or activities that can make their heart beats faster around 60 minutes per day

Adolescents (6-17 years)

It is recommended 60 minutes (1 hour) of moderate-to-vigorous level physical activity per day


Examples:-

  • Aerobic Activities

    Running and walking

  • Activities that involves muscle

    Push up, and climbing

  • Activities that involves bones

    Gymnastic

Adults (18-64 years)

It is recommended 150 minutes of moderate level physical activity per week

Also atleast 2 days in a week have activities that strengthening muscle


Examples:-

  • Aerobic Activities

    Running, walking, jogging

  • Activities that involves muscle

    Push up, and climbing, Hiking

  • Activities that involves bones

    Gymnastic

Older Adults (65 years and older)

It is recommended 150 minutes of moderate level physical activity per week

Atleast 2 days in a week have activities that strengthen muscles


Examples:-

  • Aerobic Activities

    brisk walking

The Importance of Maintain Healthy Physical Lifestyle


Improve Health and Quality of life


Many studies stated that sustain physical health could reduce diseases such as diabetes, heart attack and can affect mental health. These are chronic disease and it might affect the way of you live.  


Live Longer


Some research highlighted that, by consistently doing that physical activities can gain life expectancies. They also mention that people who tend to maintain their physical health, tend to live longer.


Stay Healthy and Active


By being active, you can do activities that some people can not do. For example hiking, jogging and many more. These activities require healthy physical strength. Maintaining a healthy physical lifestyle means do activities that match your age regularly. By staying active, it also means you will be easier to stay active when you get older.
